## What is it?

> In our Smart Country, we have a **dam** with a **reservoir** and a **river** flowing through the city.

> When there is heavy rain, the reservoir gets too full. To **protect the dam** from breaking, the flood gates must be opened to release the extra water.

> But when the gates open, water rushes into the river and can cause a **flood** in the city!

> My project is a **smart system** that watches the water level in the river and warns everyone in the city before a flood happens.

---

## How does it work?

> We have a **water sensor** in the river bed. It checks how high the water is.

> There are **3 alert levels**:

> 1. **GREEN** - Everything is safe! The water is low.
> 2. **AMBER** - Warning! The water is rising. Be careful!
> 3. **RED + SIREN** - Flood! The water is very high. Everyone must move to safety!

---

## Let me show you!

> *(Turn on the system)*

> Right now the light is **green** - the city is safe.

> The water pump is filling the reservoir... imagine this is heavy rain...

> The flood gates open and water flows into the river... watch the water rise...

> Now it turns **amber** - that means warning!

> And now... **red with a siren!** That means flood!

> The pump **automatically stops** so the water can go down.

> When the water goes back to safe level... the pump starts again.

> It keeps repeating by itself!

---

## What did I use?

> - A **Magicbit NEO** board - this is a smart computer made in Sri Lanka!
> - A **water level sensor** to measure the water
> - **3 LEDs** - green, amber, and red
> - A **buzzer** for the siren
> - A **water pump** to simulate the flood
